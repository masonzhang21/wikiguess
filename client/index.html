WikiGuessr

A game for the trivially-inclined

They say there's a Wikipedia article on every topic imaginable. How many do you think you know? Pick a category (or random, if you're particularly daring), and try your best to figure out the article title based on its content. You start with part of the intro, and you can unlock three additional sections of your choosing in the table of contents. Use your hints wisely. Guess away! And try to learn something new today!
